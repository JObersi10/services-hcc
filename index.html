Our Mission

Code that ships.
Impact that lasts.


Hack Club Curaçao is a teen-run, non-profit technology club bringing digital skills to one of the Caribbean's most vibrant islands. We learn by doing — and what we build for you is exactly how we learn.

We take on client projects that are real-world, production-grade, and meaningful. Your investment covers professional-quality digital work and sustains a community where the next generation of Caribbean engineers is being forged.

No corporate middleman. No inflated agencies. Just talented teenagers who care deeply about their craft — and their island.

Why Choose Us

Great work. Greater purpose.

Most agencies charge a premium so their executives can drive nicer cars. We charge so teenagers can learn to code.

When you hire Hack Club Curaçao, you receive professional digital services at competitive rates — and you become part of a movement to bring tech education to an island where it barely exists.

  • Transparent finances — we publish exactly how client revenue is spent each quarter.

  • Zero bureaucracy — you talk directly to the people building your product, every time.
